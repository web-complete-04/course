Recap ex 01

The user will provide 2 numbers

The program will display in separate alerts:
  1. The sum of the provided numbers (e.g. "nr1 + nr2 = sum")
  2. The product of the provided numbers (e.g. "nr1 * nr2 = sum")
  3. The value of the first number to the power of the second number (e.g. "nr1 to the power of nr2 = power")
